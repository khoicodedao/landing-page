Goto website: https://huhai-khoicodedao.vercel.app/

  1. You will see "Login with Facebook button", click on that
  2. You will be asked for "pages_show_list", "pages_read_engagement", "ads_read", "ads_management" and "business_management" permissions, allow them
  3. After allowing these permission you will be redirected to the dashboard
  4. You will see the "Businesses" button on the sidebar, click on that
  5. You will see the list of business you own along with the "Get Pages" and "Get Ad Account" button against every business
  6. By clicking on the "Get Pages" button you will see the list of Pages attach with the Business
  7. By clicking on the "Get Ad Account" link you will see the list of Ad Accounts attach with the Business
  8. By clicking on a Ad Account ID, you will see list of Ad Sets of the Ad Account
  9. You can view some information about Ad Set eg: Ad Set ID, Name and Status
  10. You also click "Active" or "Pause" button to change the Status of the Ad Set

We need these permission for the proper working of our app.
